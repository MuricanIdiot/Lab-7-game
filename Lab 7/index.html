X-Men Escape Game

__________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

The Dark Spiderman has trapped you and your fellow X-MEN! YOU MUST ESCAPE!!

DO NOT hit lava, and Collect all Spidey symbols to continue, in hopes that he will let you escape.

Choose a character and watch out for Magneto and Sabertooth.

Brought to you and pixels drawn by Cindy Haslam.

__________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

To go left, press the Left arrow key.

To go Right, press the Right arrow key.

To Jump, press the Up arrow key.
